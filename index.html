Spur Ale: A Truly Original Recipe

A smooth, rich nut brown ale with accents of dark chocolate, dark roast coffee and licorice. Made with 100% malted sorghum, Spur Ale takes inspiration and flavour from 1800s western North American frontier life where nights were often spent under the stars; a time when life was simpler and often experienced from a saddle.

Our Story

Just like a great story or laugh shared around a campfire, we believe a delicious drink becomes more than the sum of its ingredients when shared with friends and loved ones. As celiacs, we missed sharing a delicious beer with our friends and loved ones. We quickly grew tired of generic-tasting gluten free beers like they were tedious stories we had heard around the fire one too many times. Our Commitment to Gluten-Free

For some, living gluten-free is a matter of dietary choice, but for celiacs, a matter of dietary necessity. Choice or necessity, at Spur Ale, gluten-free is one of our founding values. We use 100% malted sorghum in our brewing process and never use any of our brewing and bottling equipment for brewing and bottling anything but sorghum-based brews. This allows us to gaurantee with 100% certainty that our Ale does not (and will never) contain gluten! Also, unlike most beers with a "chocolate" flavour that simply use a barley-based, gluten-containing chocolate malt, we actually use 100% gluten-free 72% dark chocolate, along with 100% gluten-free salted licorice and 100% gluten-free fresh ground dark roast coffee.
